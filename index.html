About Us

About TakaCycle

TakaCycle was founded in Nairobi after urban planner Zawadi Mwenda noticed how poor waste management was affecting communities. Inspired by successful recycling reward systems in countries like Germany and South Korea, she teamed up with a small group of creatives to bring a local, tech-driven solution to life. TakaCycle is rooted in the belief that even small recycling actions can create big change when powered by people.

“We didn't want to just manage waste. We wanted to create a movement.” - Zawadi Mwenda, Founder

TakaCycle is a community-based recycling rewards platform that turns everyday waste into real value. Through a mobile app and smart drop-off bins, users can track recyclables, earn EcoPoints and redeem them for discounts at local businesses.
